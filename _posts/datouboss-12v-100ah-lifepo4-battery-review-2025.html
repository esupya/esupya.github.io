FAQ Section

Can I use this battery to start my vehicle's engine?

No, this is a deep cycle battery designed for sustained power delivery, not the high cranking amps required for engine starting. Use it for accessories, appliances, and electronics, but keep your vehicle's starting battery separate.

Do I need a special charger for this lithium battery?

Yes, you must use a lithium-specific charger that provides the correct charging profile (constant current/constant voltage) with appropriate voltage limits. Using a lead-acid charger will likely damage the battery and void warranty.

How many batteries can I connect together?

You can connect up to 4 batteries in series (for 48V systems) or parallel (for 400Ah capacity). For combined series/parallel configurations, consult the manual for specific guidelines.

What's the actual usable capacity?

With LiFePO4 chemistry, you can safely use 80-90% of the rated capacity (80-90Ah) compared to 50% with lead-acid batteries. This effectively doubles your usable power compared to similarly rated lead-acid batteries.
