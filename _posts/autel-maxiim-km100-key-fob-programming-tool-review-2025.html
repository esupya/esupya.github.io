Frequently Asked Questions

Does the KM100 work with other brands of keys or chips?

The KM100 primarily works with Autel's own keys and chips. For chip generation and cloning, it supports the Autel AT100 super chip. For physical keys, it works with Autel's universal keys (slave keys). This ensures compatibility and reliability in programming operations.

How do I activate the KM100 after purchase?

Activation requires creating an Autel account online and binding your KM100 device to that account. The process is similar to other Autel tablet devices and typically takes only a few minutes. Most users find the activation straightforward with the provided instructions.

Are updates really free for life?

Yes, Autel currently offers lifetime free updates for the KM100. This means you'll receive all software updates and new vehicle coverage additions without additional charges, which is exceptional value compared to competitors who charge annual subscription fees.

What vehicles are NOT supported?

The KM100 doesn't natively support some regional vehicles including Australian Fords and Holdens, Malaysian Peroduas and Protons, Brazilian Fiats and GMs, and Indian Mahindras and Tatas. However, support for these can be added for an additional fee after purchase.

How long does the battery last during continuous use?

The 4950mAh lithium-polymer battery typically provides 6-8 hours of continuous use, which covers a full day of programming jobs for most professional users. The device can also be used while charging if needed for extended sessions.
